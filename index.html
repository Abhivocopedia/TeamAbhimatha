About Team Abhimatha

Serving the community since 2010

Team Abhimatha operates under the Moodugiliyaru Janaseva Trust as a youth-led initiative focused on social and environmental activism in the Udupi district.

Our mission is to create sustainable impact through community engagement, environmental conservation, and social advocacy.
